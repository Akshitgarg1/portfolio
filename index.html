    My Story

    From Curiosity to Code

    It all started with a simple question — "How do apps work?" That curiosity, sparked during my school days, turned into a passion as I explored programming for the first time. I still remember the excitement when I wrote my first "Hello World" in C.

    College gave wings to my curiosity. I started working on real-world projects, from basic web pages to full-stack applications. Every project wasn't just code — it was a challenge, a lesson, and a step closer to the developer I aspire to be.

    I've faced bugs I couldn't fix, concepts I couldn't grasp at first, and moments of self-doubt. But I also discovered the joy of solving problems, building things that work, and learning how much I love tech.

    Today, I'm not just a student of Computer Science — I'm a lifelong learner. And this portfolio? It's not just a showcase — it's my story in motion.
